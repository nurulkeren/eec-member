English Day

English Day is a weekly activity where members practice speaking English through games, discussions, roleplay, presentations, and interactive challenges.

Benefits

  • Improve speaking confidence.
  • Practice pronunciation.
  • Expand vocabulary.
  • Meet new friends.
×

English Class

English Class is a regular learning program designed to improve members' English skills through interactive lessons, discussions, speaking practice, grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and fun classroom activities. Members can learn in a supportive environment while building confidence in using English for academic and daily communication.

Benefits

  • Improve speaking confidence.
  • Strengthen grammar and vocabulary.
  • Develop listening and communication skills.
  • Learn together in a supportive environment.
×

Toefl Class

TOEFL Class helps members prepare for the TOEFL test by learning strategies and practicing Listening, Structure & Written Expression, and Reading Comprehension. Members will become familiar with TOEFL question types while improving their overall English proficiency.

Benefits

  • Understand the TOEFL test format.
  • Improve TOEFL scores.
  • Learn effective test-taking strategies.
×

Passion of Debate

Passion of Debate is a program where members learn the fundamentals of English debating, including argument building, rebuttal techniques, critical thinking, and public speaking. Members practice through debates, simulations, and constructive feedback to develop confidence and analytical skills.

Benefits

  • Improve public speaking skills.
  • Develop critical and analytical thinking.
  • Learn to build strong arguments.
×

Calling Delegates

Calling Delegate is a program that provides information and invitations for members to participate in English competitions, conferences, seminars, Model United Nations (MUN), and other academic events. Members receive updates and encouragement to explore opportunities beyond campus.

Benefits

  • Discover national and international opportunities.
  • Gain competition and organizational experience.
  • Strengthen your CV and portfolio.
×

Internship

Internship offers members the opportunity to become part of the Engineering English Club committee and actively contribute to its programs and events. Through hands-on experience, members develop leadership, teamwork, communication, and organizational skills while supporting the growth of the club.

Benefits

  • Gain real organizational experience.
  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills.
  • Build professional relationships with fellow members.
